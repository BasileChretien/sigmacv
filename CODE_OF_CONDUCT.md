# Code of Conduct

SigmaCV adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as its code of conduct.
We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment
for everyone who participates in this project — contributors, maintainers, and
users alike.

## Our standard

The full text of the standard we follow is maintained canonically by the
Contributor Covenant project and is incorporated here by reference:

- **Contributor Covenant v2.1:**
  <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>
- Translations are available at
  <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/translations/>.

In short: treat others with empathy and respect, assume good faith, and help
keep this a community where people of every background can contribute safely.

## Scope

This code applies within all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull
requests, discussions — and in public spaces when an individual is representing
the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a concern, please report it privately to the
maintainer at **chretien.basile.jean.bernard.u4@s.mail.nagoya-u.ac.jp**. Reports
are handled confidentially. The maintainer is responsible for reviewing and
responding to reports and may take any action deemed appropriate, up to and
including removing a contributor from the project's spaces.
