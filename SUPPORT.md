# Getting help

Thanks for your interest in SigmaCV! Here's how to get help or get in touch — no technical knowledge required.

## I have a question, or my CV looks wrong

- **Ask a question or share feedback** — open a [Feedback or question](https://github.com/BasileChretien/sigmacv/issues/new?template=feedback.yml) issue. Tell us in your own words; you don't need to know any code.
- For open-ended conversation, use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/BasileChretien/sigmacv/discussions).
- Many common questions are answered in the [FAQ](docs/FAQ.md).

## I found a bug

- Open a [Bug report](https://github.com/BasileChretien/sigmacv/issues/new?template=bug_report.yml).

## I'd like to suggest a feature, or a new CV layout

- Use the feedback form above, or — if you're technical — open a pull request (see [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)). Translations into more languages are especially welcome.

## Security

Please do **not** open a public issue for a security problem. See [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for how to report it privately.

---

_SigmaCV is maintained not-for-profit, so replies may take a little time — thank you for your patience._
